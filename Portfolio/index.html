About me...

Hello, my name is Rachel Conforti. I am a Florida native that moved to Charlotte in 2017.

I am graduating University of North Carolina at Charlotte in December of 2021. I am a part of the early entry program so I will be graduating with half my Masters in Data Science as well. I am an active member in the Association of Machinery and Girl's Who Code organizations on campus.

I have had a long winding road that has lead me here today. I started off my college career with the intent of becoming a doctor. But I quickly realize I throughly disliked learning about biology. So, I quickly had to piviot to what I enjoyed the most and landed on a Mathematics degree. I only began to question my degree again when I was constantly meeting computer science students and hearing about the cool things they were learning and doing in class. So, I decided since I kept thinking about it I would take the beginner coding course at the college and I instantly felt like I found my calling. I have always played on computers, my father introduced me to video games when I was very little. So, I spent the majority of my childhood on a computer. Looking back I find it funny that I never thought about a career in technology when that is what I spent most of my life interacting with, but as they say hindsight is 20/20. However, I am very lucky to be in my postion today, excited to gradute soon and growing ever more excited with each step.
