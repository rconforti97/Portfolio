About me...

Hello! My name is Rachel Conforti. I am working as currently a Software Engineer at U.S. Bank. I started my career working at MUFG Union Bank in June of 2022. While gaining hands-on experience I finished my Master of Science in Computer Science in August of 2022 with a 3.9 GPA.

Majority of my work while at MUFG Union Bank was geared towards getting our application ready for my team's application migration to U.S. Bank. My team is an agile-style team that uses Jira. A few things I have done that I am proud of is creating custom automation python scripts that query our SQL database to allow our database to be transferred to U.S. Bank with a single command. I have also worked on resolving multiple security flaws in our front and backend code using Veracode. Our code went from 55 security flaws ranging from critical to medium to zero.

What I love about this field is the constant opportunities for learning. I love the ability to work with my team to try to figure out complex problems. If you wish to see my resume please click the link below!

Greedy and A* Search

This was for my Artificial Intelligence course we were asked to build a greedy and A* search using python.
