About me...

Hello, my name is Rachel Conforti. I am currently a Senior at University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I am a part of the Early Entry program meaning I will be graduating in December of 2021 with my undergraduate in Computer Science with a concentration in Data Science, and my minor in Mathematics along with half of my Masters in Data Science completed.

I chose computer science as my profession because it was the first major that combined what I love the most, creating and computers. I have always enjoyed creating, crafting, and coloring while simultaneously being on the computer and playing video games. These two sides of myself have always dueled, if I was doing one I couldn't do the other. So, when I decided to switch my major to computer science I finally felt that my need for both were satisfied. I throughly love the idea that I can sit in front of the computer and create whatever I would like to create.

This field is so diverse with so many opportunites. I have an idea of where I would want to go but I am looking foward to the journey and seeing what becomes avaliable to me. If you are interested in more of my skills; my resume is linked below.

World Happiness Data Visualization

Using Dash and Plotly we created 3 different interactive graphs. We have a chloropleth, a stacked bar chart, and a multi-dot graph at the bottom. We also have a slider and a drop-down box all created in python. This was a group effort in which none of us have used dash and plotly so it was very fun to me to watch us grow through out the project and create something I think is very interesting to look at.

Greedy and A* Search

This was for my Artificial Intelligence course we were asked to build a greedy and A* search using python. We had the program read in the grid from a file with the start node and goal node pre-defined and find the optimal route depnding if you selected Greedy or A* search. It returns with starting node, the goal node, the final path executed, and the number states expanded.
